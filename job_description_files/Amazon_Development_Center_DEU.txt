Job summary
Amazon Monitron is a brand new AWS service offering end to end predictive maintenance solution including the sensor and gateway devices. Monitron is part of AWS Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI-ML) organisation, bringing the power of ML to new application fields.
Amazon Monitron is looking for a Software Development Engineer who is equally passionate about the technology and technical challenges in software development as collaborating with business and other domain experts (firmware, hardware, science and UX) and communicating with business teams to enable the success of the end to end solution for our customers. Our technical challenges involve low latency back-end software architecture, edge computing, applied science, and digital signal processing. We develop and apply quantitative algorithms (e.g. machine learning, computer vision) to solve real-world problems and applications at global scale.
