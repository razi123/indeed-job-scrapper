the role & the team
as a senior backend engineer - address validation in our tech logistics department, you will be responsible for defining and delivering a high-performance, stable and scalable system to validate the customers address for safe delivery and also fraud check. you will have a direct impact on our customers' happiness. we aim to delight our customers and make the zalando customer experience as convenient as possible. you will support the teams with experience and best practices to find the best solutions, review and challenge solution proposals, and make sure that our products remain extensible in the future.
