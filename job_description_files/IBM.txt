introduction
the turbonomic brand sales specialist will be directly responsible for driving client-facing sales and client engagement activities to accelerate the adoption and deployment of turbonomic offerings for their assigned territory/accounts. application resource management is one of the key focus topics in ibm's automation portfolio and represents a critical part in the area of aiops solutions.

your role and responsibilities
as a brand sales specialist you play an essential role in accelerating ibm technology portfolio successful in the market. we want you to specifically drive the success of turbonomic application resource management as an integrated part of the ibm automation solutions. as part of an agile and enthusiastic team you take over the following responsibilities:
generate demand, qualify and progress client opportunities, and negotiate pricing and contracts
serve as the turbonomic sales expert to drive the strategy, sale, and closure of turbonomic offerings and use cases
develop and maintain relationships with the highest levels of customer management across executive, line of business, and it
maintain and orchestrate account specific plans and strategies
work with prospects and customers to prove the value of the turbonomic solutions and its relation to the aiops set of technologies
achieve assigned revenue and signings goals for turbonomic products for assigned territory/accounts
provide timely and accurate pipeline and forecast updates
work collaboratively in a highly matrixed environment across ibm
maintain contemporary technology skills and offering knowledge in relation to application resource management and/or network performance management and adjacent technologies

required technical and professional expertise
extensive experience with virtualization technology (esp. vmware)
understanding of the underlying open source and concepts around storage, networking, security, trouble shooting, management
knowledge of building and operating clusters effectively on a kubernetes platform
cloud platform knowledge and in one of the following: microsoft azure, google cloud platform/anthos, amazon/aws, ibm public cloud
understanding of containers and microservices application architecture
strong linux skills
knowledge of the role of traditional it operations (itops) engineers, tools they are typically using and pain points they are facing
understanding of how a site reliability engineer (sre) approaches operations to ensure that continuously delivered applications run efficiently and reliably by using software engineering and automation solutions
ability to assess where the client is on the journey from traditional it operations to sre, and able to articulate and demonstrate the value of ibm turbonomic to help them with the transition. ibm will provide turbonomic specific educations and trainings

preferred technical and professional expertise
knowledge of adjacent ibm technologies including ibm cloudpak for watson aiops
knowledge of cloud-native technologies and hybrid cloud architectures
knowledge of application centric it operations
